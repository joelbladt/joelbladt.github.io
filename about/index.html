Hello, I’m Joel Bladt.

Since the age of eleven, when I received my first computer, my enthusiasm for technology has been unstoppable. In addition to playing on my computer, I wrote my first small but amusing programs in Visual Basic at a young age, aiming to shut down other computers on the network – at least that's how I remember it. Later on, my interest shifted towards websites.

I successfully completed my training as an IT specialist in a reputable insurance agency. After graduating, I found my professional home in an event agency, where I spent two years implementing websites for various purposes based on their in-house developed CMS.

Currently, I am employed in a company specializing in the maintenance and advancement of bodybuilder systems for renowned car brands. I work on various microservices based on the Laravel framework. It's fascinating to be part of a team that continuously strives for the perfection and enhancement of these systems.
